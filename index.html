Our philosophy

When you last time make a new friend? When you last time had a friendly meeting with interesting people?

let’s return «live» communication

The answer is the same for all of us. The rapid rhythm of life and multiple online services have taken us away from «live» communication. CrossLine is designed to bring back these warm, real moments. Crossline will remind you how make real friends and have casual conversation in a pleasant atmosphere.

Crossline - intersection of lines

We erase the boundaries between the virtual and physical worlds. This we call the intersection of lines — Crossline.
